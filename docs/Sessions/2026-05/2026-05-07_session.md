## What was learned

- **Net-new MEDIUM finding from the correlation matrix:** the 6 active edges are NOT one-strategy-with-extra-trades. The 4 fundamentals show moderate cross-correlation (≈0.51 in the value family) but the 2 technical edges are well-decorrelated from them. This rules out the "ensemble is collinear" hypothesis without needing signal-level instrumentation.
- **Bootstrap CI test design gotcha:** for "p_above_zero ≈ 0.5 on zero-mean returns" you must explicitly center the series (`rets - rets.mean()`) — the seeded RNG draws have non-zero sample means by chance, so the bootstrap distribution centers on the realized point Sharpe, not on 0.
- **Embargo-vs-no-embargo geometry was load-bearing.** Every Sharpe in the falsification record was measured with `test_start = full_timeline[train_end_idx]` — features computed at train_end overlap test_start by up to ~max-lookback days. Future falsification numbers will be slightly tighter than historical ones; this is a feature, not a bug.
